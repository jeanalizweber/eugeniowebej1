phishing: a real online danger

phishing is one of the most common online scams today. it happens when someone pretends to be a trusted company or person in order to steal your private information, like passwords or credit card numbers. phishing emails can look very real, which is what makes them so dangerous.

these scams often use real company logos, urgent messages, and personal details to trick people into clicking links or giving away information. even careful users can sometimes fall for phishing if they do not know the warning signs.

types of phishing attacks

  • spear phishing – targeted emails made to look personal and convincing
  • whaling – phishing aimed at high-level executives or important individuals
  • smishing – phishing through text messages
  • vishing – phishing through phone calls

phishing is a serious threat, but learning how it works is the first step to staying safe online.
